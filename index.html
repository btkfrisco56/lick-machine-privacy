Lick Machine — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Overview

Lick Machine ("the App") is developed by BK Guitars. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how the App works.

Data We Collect

The App collects minimal data to function:

Lick generation parameters: When you generate a lick, your selected key, scale, style, era, difficulty, tempo, and guitar configuration are sent to our server to generate the lick. This data is not stored on our server after the response is returned.

Subscription status: If you subscribe to Player or Pro, Apple handles all payment processing. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us:

Your saved lick library, guitar configurations, app preferences, and debug logs (if enabled). This data stays on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

Anthropic Claude API: Lick generation requests are processed through Anthropic's Claude API via our server. The musical parameters you select are sent to generate licks. No personal information is included in these requests. Anthropic's privacy policy can be found at anthropic.com/privacy.

Apple StoreKit: Subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your Apple ID, payment method, or billing information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, browsing history, device identifiers, or any personal information. We do not use analytics or tracking tools. We do not serve advertisements.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@bkguitars.com.
